# Automate WAX Blockchain Operations with Our New n8n Community Node

We're excited to announce the release of n8n-nodes-wax, a powerful new community node that brings WAX blockchain automation to n8n workflows. Built by Velocity BPA, this node opens up endless possibilities for NFT creators, marketplace operators, and blockchain gaming platforms.

## The Challenge

The WAX blockchain has become a cornerstone of the NFT and gaming ecosystem, hosting millions of digital assets and transactions daily. However, managing NFTs, monitoring AtomicAssets, and integrating marketplace operations often requires manual intervention or custom code that's time-consuming to develop and maintain.

Developers and business operators need a way to automate their WAX blockchain operations without writing extensive code or managing complex API integrations.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-wax

Our new community node seamlessly integrates WAX blockchain functionality into your n8n workflows, enabling you to automate NFT management, AtomicAssets operations, marketplace activities, and gaming integrations with a visual, no-code approach.

## Key Features

- **NFT Management**: Create, transfer, and manage NFTs programmatically within your workflows
- **AtomicAssets Integration**: Access and manipulate AtomicAssets data with ease
- **Marketplace Operations**: Automate listing, pricing, and sales monitoring
- **Gaming Support**: Build automated workflows for blockchain gaming mechanics
- **Visual Workflow Builder**: Leverage n8n's intuitive interface to connect WAX operations with 400+ other services

## Real-World Use Cases

Imagine automatically minting NFTs when new artwork is uploaded to your storage, sending notifications when marketplace sales occur, or triggering in-game rewards based on blockchain events. With n8n-nodes-wax, these workflows become simple drag-and-drop operations.

## Getting Started

Installing the WAX node is straightforward. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-wax


After restarting n8n, you'll find the WAX node available in your node palette, ready to integrate into your workflows.
